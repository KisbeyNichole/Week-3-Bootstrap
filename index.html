About me

Im a 22 year old artist trying to navigate the world, I like DND, anime, Ravens, Foxes, and Ravens. one day i aspire to be a visual designer but until then im just learning and gaining skill tree points as i level through life.

Goals

One day i want to own my own comic studio or video game studio, while i love visual design, it would be nice to use those skills and ideas to gear a group of people to help me bring everything to life.

Projects
